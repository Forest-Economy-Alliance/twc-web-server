About Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering

VISION

To nurture the students as a technocrat with new ideas and innovations in the field of Electronics and Communication Engineering for the benefit of industry and society in the state and the country as a whole.

MISSION

To setup the department as a centre of excellence in the area of Electronics and Communication Engineering with focus on:

  • M1. To develop as a quality academic centre in order to produce students with professional.
  • M2. To motivate and encourage the students for nucleation of new ideas and innovation by creating research environment for the benefit of the society.
  • M3. To develop the ability to understand the ethical values of research and development and adaptability towards recent trends in technology.
